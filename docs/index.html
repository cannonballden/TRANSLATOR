Tips for best results
  • Use short clips (5–20 seconds), <= 10 MB to avoid proxy limits.
  • Prefer WAV audio or common video (mp4/webm). Install ffmpeg in Codespaces for more formats.
  • Keep the animal visible and the mic close; avoid heavy wind and crowd noise.
  • The summary is heuristic: treat as guidance, not truth.
